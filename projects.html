KeyPress Game

Technology Used:HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MERN Stack

A website which educates a 2-3 year old kid. It includes Alphabets and numbers. If the user presses any key on the keyboard example 'p' two images(image-1: Displaying letter p and image-2: Displaying a parrot) will pop and an audio will play 'p for parrot'.

    The features it includes are as follows:
  • Login/Signup for parents
  • Audio description for the kid to learn faster
  • Parenting guide to educate their kid.
  • Fun learning for the children.

    • Smart Car for Farm

    Technology Used: Internet of Things

    It was a group project in which we buit a smart car having some features which will make it easier for farmers to water their farm/crops or sprinkle any pesticides.

      The features it included are as follows:
  • Soil moisture sensor to keep track of soil moisture and water that area of farm where the soil moisture level is low.
  • A pump on the top to sprikle water.
  • Ultrasonic Sensor to detect any obstacle in front and change its direction accordingly.
